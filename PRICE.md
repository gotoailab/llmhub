## Pricing Notes

1. **Volume Discounts:** Many providers offer discounts for high-volume usage. Contact sales for enterprise pricing.

2. **Free Tiers:** Several providers offer free tiers or trial credits:
   - OpenAI: Limited free tier
   - Google: Free tier with limits
   - DeepSeek: 1M tokens free (90 days)
   - Moonshot: 1M tokens free (90 days)
   - Baichuan: 1M tokens free (90 days)
   - MINIMAX: 1M tokens free (90 days)

3. **Tiered Pricing:** Some models (like GLM) use tiered pricing based on input token count.

4. **Batch Processing:** Some providers offer lower rates for batch inference (e.g., DeepSeek).

5. **Context Windows:** Larger context windows may have different pricing structures.

6. **Regional Pricing:** Domestic Chinese providers may have different pricing for Chinese vs. international customers.
